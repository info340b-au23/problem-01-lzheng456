Zack Fair

Zack is a character from the Final Fantasy series. He was born in the fictional town of Gongaga and dreamed of becoming a SOLDIER. He later achieves this under the mentorship of Angeal Hewley, a 1st Class SOLDIER. Zack is portrayed as optimistic and outgoing, and is usually seen carrying a large sword called the Buster Sword.
